How to play & the strategy behind the three dynamics

Each turn you collect Gold equal to your Economy, then take one action. The enemy AI does the same. First to knock the opponent's Base from 30 to 0 wins.

Economy is the engine — more gold every turn, forever. But a turn spent on economy is a turn not spent defending, so a pure economy player is exposed to an early rush.

Military (Train Troops + Attack) is the only way to deal damage. Each troop has 1 power. Attacking is aggressive tempo, but defenders have the edge: when armies clash, the attacker must overpower the defender just to break even, and only the overflow strength reaches the Base.

Research multiplies your army — +1 attack and +1 defense to every troop. It's slow and useless without an army, but it's what turns a small force into a decisive one.

The balance: Economy beats a slow opponent, a rush beats greedy economy, and Research beats a big-but-primitive army. Read your opponent and counter their plan — that's the chess of it.
